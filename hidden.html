Swimming

As the sayings goes, you shouldn't swim for up to 30 minutes after you eat. I found that out the hard way. Cramping is no fun when swimming in thirty-five degree water. Lake Michigan in March is not the best time to go for a swim. Even with the sun shining and warm weather, the lake stays relentlessly cold. If you have to go swimming, wait until summer. If you happen to eat a few boardwalk hotdogs, you should wait 30 minutes.

Lamps

Talk about a bright idea, lamps have brought a 24/7 lifestyle into our lives. Due to the finding of electricity and subsequent invention of the lightbulb, we are able to live well past the sun setting. The lamp and the technology behind it is one of last century's greatest inventions.
